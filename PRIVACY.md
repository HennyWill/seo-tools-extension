# Privacy Policy for SEO Tools Chrome Extension

**Last Updated: December 2025**

## Overview

SEO Tools is a Chrome extension developed by HennyWill. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Data Collection

**We do not collect any personal data.**

SEO Tools operates entirely locally in your browser. The extension:

- Does NOT collect any personal information
- Does NOT track your browsing activity
- Does NOT send any data to external servers
- Does NOT use analytics or tracking tools
- Does NOT store any data outside your browser

## Data Storage

The only data stored by this extension is your theme preference (light/dark mode), which is saved locally in your browser using localStorage. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is not accessible to us or any third party
- Can be cleared by clearing your browser data

## Permissions

The extension requests the following permissions:

- **activeTab**: Required to analyze the current page when you click on extension buttons. This permission only activates when you interact with the extension.
- **scripting**: Required to execute content scripts that analyze page content (headings, links, meta tags, etc.)

These permissions are used solely to provide the extension's functionality and do not enable any data collection.

## Third-Party Services

When you use certain features, the extension opens links to third-party services:

- Google Search (google.com)
- Google Search Console (search.google.com)
- Ahrefs (ahrefs.com)
- Archive.org (web.archive.org)
- Google Rich Results Test (search.google.com/test/rich-results)

These services have their own privacy policies. We recommend reviewing them if you have concerns about how they handle your data.

## Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this document.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or the extension, you can:

## Summary

**SEO Tools does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data. Your privacy is fully protected.**
